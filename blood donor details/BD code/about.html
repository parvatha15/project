World Blood Donor Day

The world blood donors day is celebrated every year "June 14".The world blood donor day was establised by the WHO.The World Blood Donors Day is celebrated on the birth anniversary of a Nobel Prize Scientist Karl Landsteiner.The main objective of this Day is to create awareness of the importance of human blood.
